Our Services

MH Williams Construction has several LEED certified employees, which allows us to build structures that emphasize environmental efficiencies. LEED stands for leadership in energy and environmental design. LEED is a rating system for buildings. Under LEED, buildings receive scores for saving energy, and limiting storm water runoff. When the points are added up, the building earns a LEED rating. The higher the score, the more sustainable the building.

LEED certification is important because it provides confirmation that a structure was designed and built using methods that reduce energy and water usage, promote better indoor air quality, and improve the quality of our lives.

The benefits of LEED Certification include:

# A decrease in the use of energy and water

# Less maintenance and lower operation costs

# Better indoor air quality

# an upgrade in the quality of life for all
